Australian Domestic Tourism in 2025

Explore how tourism activity varied across Australian states, travel purposes and expenditure categories throughout 2025.

Where does domestic tourism generate the most value in Australia?

Tourism expenditure across Australian states during Q4 2025.

New South Wales consistently generated the highest tourism expenditure throughout 2025, followed by Victoria and Queensland. This highlights the dominance of Australia's most populous states in driving domestic tourism spending.

Tourism Expenditure by State and Quarter

Smaller jurisdictions such as Tasmania, the Northern Territory and the Australian Capital Territory contributed a much smaller share of national tourism expenditure.

Change in Tourism Expenditure from Q1 to Q4

Several states experienced stronger spending in the December quarter (Q4), suggesting increased tourism activity during the holiday season. Meanwhile Tasmania and Victoria has experienced a negative change in expenditure, this shows that these states are not highly visited during the holiday season

Why do Australians travel?

Change in travel purpose over time

Holiday travel was the most common purpose throughout 2025 and showed steady growth across all four quarters.

Business travel declined significantly over the year, suggesting that leisure travel played a much larger role in domestic tourism demand than work-related travel.

Expenditure of travel purpose over time

Even though the number of "other" trips were higher than "Business" trips, Business trips have had more expenditure than other trips, mainly due to higher expenses

Tourism Activity by State

Visitor Nights vs Domestic Trips by State

How efficiently do states convert tourism into revenue?

Average spending of tourist per trip by states

Some states generated significantly more expenditure per trip than others, indicating differences in tourist spending behaviour and travel costs.

Average spending of tourist per night by states

Higher spending per night indicates that certain destinations are able to capture more economic value from each visitor stay.

What do tourists actually spend their money on?

Tourism Expenditure by Categories

Accommodation, restaurant meals and petrol accounted for the largest categories of tourism expenditure in 2025, which are essential expenses during a trip.
